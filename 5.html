I dreamed that my mom was gone. I think she passed away but everyone hid it from me. I was searching for her in a frenzy.


When I woke up, I thought about what she said to me when I was home over the winter, “If your parents are still alive—even if it’s only one of them—you’ll always feel like a child. You're entitled to that feeling. But once they’re both gone, you can never come home again. There's no more comfort or protection, only somewhere for you to disappear into.”
