I dreamed that it was the end of the world and everyone needed to play hard to survive. Setting looked like my primary school. My mom and my grandparents were locked in a building that I escaped from. They were chilling on the third floor balcony even though they were about to be purged if they lost the games (rounds and rounds of survival competitions, cue Saw). My grandma even winked at me when we made eye contact. I headed into the music building with someone who wanted to kill me, I don't know if I managed to get rid of them but through an all-knowing perspective I saw my family beating a big red female Santa in a challenge. It was gruesome but I was relieved. I woke up.
